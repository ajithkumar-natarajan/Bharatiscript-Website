Sudent ambassador programme

A Student Ambassador is the representative of Bharati script in their college. It is his/her responsibility to connect with and reach out to the students who are enthusiastic about learning and contributing to achieving our targets on their respective campuses.

What's your job?

Publicity

  • Conduct small outreach programs about Bharati script within college campus, nearby schools and take it to public in any imaginative way.
  • Display the posters of Bharati script within two to three days of receiving them on college bulletins and notice boards.

Communicate

  • Bridge the gap between other students/persons who show interest in Bharati script and the team Bharati for exchange of Queries and information. Update the students about our new ideas and targets of our work through the mails and other modes of communication.

Share

  • Publicizing the updates of work on Bharati on various social media platforms like Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, Whatsapp sharing the posts of the event.

Frequently asked questions

  • A letter of excellence from Indian Institute of Technology, Madras will be issued exclusively for the category of student ambassador.
  • An opportunity to improve your communication and managerial skills by interacting with people of diverse fields and from various background across India.
  • Top 5 student ambassadors will receive 100GB Google drive storage space with validity through 2019.
  • Click here and submit your application form. A No Objection Certificate (NOC) given by your HOD/Principal with the subject of the letter as BS-SA application should be uploaded in the Google form. The same should also serve as ‘conduct certificate’. Please understand that we cannot afford to take those whose conduct is not certified by ‘responsible authority’.
  • Within 7 days from then you should submit a small assignment written in bharati script (this will be used to assess your mastery over the script).
  • If you qualify, you are on board ‘as the ambassador’ for your campus!
  • Completion of every task, as mentioned above (under What's your job?), on time would keep you on list of contenders for the top positions.
  • Based on the online and offline reach of Bharati script.

More rules may be added whenever it is felt that there is a need. The same will be intimated through e-mail.
